# Glossary — CourseLLM (LLMRnD)

- **Socratic Chatbot** — Conversational AI that teaches by asking guiding questions.  
- **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** — Combining document retrieval with AI text generation.  
- **Topic** — A unit of learning (e.g., “Recursion Basics”).  
- **Practice Question** — A short exercise tied to a topic for self-assessment.  
- **Mastery Label** — “Needs Practice”, “Getting There”, or “Strong”.  
- **Vector Database** — Database for semantic embeddings used in RAG.  
- **Anonymized Analytics** — Statistics shown to teachers without student identifiers.  
- **LLM-Resilient Assignment** — Exercises that encourage reasoning, even when AI tools are used.
