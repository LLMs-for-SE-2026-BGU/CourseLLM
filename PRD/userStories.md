# User Stories

## Personas

- **Student** – enrolled in a CS course; seeks help, practice, and clarity.
- **Teacher** – curates materials; designs exams/quizzes; monitors progress.

## Student Stories

- As a Student, I want to **ask questions restricted to course content**, so I don’t get misleading or off-topic answers.
- As a Student, I want **Socratic guidance** that uncovers my misconceptions before giving answers, so I truly learn.
- As a Student, I want to **generate practice quizzes** on selected topics to check mastery.
- As a Student, I want the assistant to **cite sources (slides/notes)** for every answer, so I can verify.

## Teacher Stories

- As a Teacher, I want to **upload and index course files** and whitelist external references.
- As a Teacher, I want to **define learning trajectories** with topic prerequisites.
- As a Teacher, I want to **auto-generate assignments/exams** aligned to the syllabus and validate testability/length.
- As a Teacher, I want to **monitor student progress** and identify knowledge gaps.
- As a Teacher, I want to **detect potential cheating** or AI misuse patterns.

## Acceptance Hints (MVP)

- Each student answer includes at least one **approved citation**.
- Quiz generation supports **#questions**, **difficulty**, and **topics** filters.
- Teacher can upload PDFs/MD and see them **indexed** within minutes.
